## Tianqi Chen (天麒 陈)

I am currently a third-year PhD student in Statistics advised by [Prof. Mingyuan Zhou](https://mingyuanzhou.github.io/) in the Department of *Information, Risk, and Operations Management* (**IROM**) at Red McCombs School of Business, *University of Texas at Austin*. Before coming to Austin, I spent two wonderful years doing my master's at the *University of Michigan, Ann Arbor*, where I was honored to work with [Prof. Ji Zhu](https://dept.stat.lsa.umich.edu/~jizhu/) on *fraud detection* and [Prof. Alfred Hero](https://hero.engin.umich.edu/) on *adversarial robustness*. I received my B.S. in Applied Mathematics from Fudan University in Shanghai, 2019. Recently, I have been focused on *deep generative modeling* and its application on multimodal data.
